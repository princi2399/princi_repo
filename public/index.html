How It Works

Four-stage pipeline from data ingestion to actionable alert delivery.

📡
Step 1

Data Collection

Ingest live transactional and performance data from PayU's ecosystem.

🧠
Step 2

Anomaly Detection

ML and rule-based logic detect deviations in success rates and error patterns.

🔔
Step 3

Alert Generation

Generate contextual alerts with affected entities, duration, and severity.

📨
Step 4

Notification Delivery

Send alerts via webhook, email, or Teams for automated response.

Key Monitoring Capabilities

Comprehensive detection and alerting across the entire payment stack.

📈

Transaction Success Rate

Continuously tracks success rate metrics across merchants and banks. Detects deviations and performance degradation.

🔍

Failure Pattern Detection

Identifies sudden spikes in failure rates. Traces root causes like gateway downtimes or configuration issues.

⚡

Real-Time Anomaly Detection

Statistical and behavioral models capture issues before merchants escalate. Contextualizes with srt_drop metrics.

🏢

Multi-Entity Monitoring

Alert at merchant, acquirer, card scheme, or product level with selective subscriptions.

🎯

Severity Classification

All alerts scored 0–100. Low (0–30), Medium (31–60), High (61–100) for prioritized investigation.

💚

Continuous Health Tracking

Tracks alert duration, auto-resolves when performance normalcy is restored. Enables benchmarking.

Use Cases & Scenarios

      
Real-world scenarios where Overwatch alerts drive operational decisions.

      

        

          
📈

          
Merchant Performance Degradation

          
A merchant's transaction success rate decreases compared to historical data.

          
Trigger: Metric: "success_rate" with criticality score ranging low to high.

        

        

          
🚨

          
Zero Success Rate Warning

          
All merchant transactions fail, leading to a zero success rate.

          
Trigger: stats.zero_srt: true and criticality_score ≥ 60.

        

        

          
📊

          
SLA Compliance & Reporting

          
Teams require data on uptime and anomaly durations across merchants.

          
Trigger: Anomaly start/end times (started_at, ended_at) notifications.

        

        

          
🔀

          
Intelligent Alert Routing

          
Dedicated teams manage specific merchants or product lines.

          
Trigger: Payload entity_type and product fields for routing.
